PAWS of Austin – Animal Rescue Website Redesign

UX/UI Case Study

Overview

PAWS of Austin is a nonprofit animal rescue organization dedicated to helping abandoned, injured, and displaced animals. The goal of this redesign was to strengthen the organization’s online presence, improve adoptability visibility, and create an easier, more welcoming experience for potential adopters, donors, and volunteers.

My Role

  • UX/UI Designer
  • Information Architecture
  • Wireframing & Layout Design
  • Visual Branding

Problem

The original PAWS of Austin website was outdated, visually inconsistent, and hard to navigate. Important content—such as available dogs, adoption information, and donation options—was difficult to find. As a result, users experienced confusion and the organization missed opportunities for support.

Solution

The redesigned experience introduces clear navigation, updated visual branding, and improved accessibility. The new structure highlights adoptable pets, explains the adoption process, and streamlines donations to increase support for the organization’s mission.

Wireframes

Outcome

The updated design established a warm and trustworthy feel, making it easier for users to connect emotionally with the dogs and the mission. The improved layout and simplified information flow create a smoother experience for adopters and supporters.
