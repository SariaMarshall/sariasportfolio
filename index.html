Case Studies

Centered Mind Counseling

The Centered Mind Counseling website redesign focused on capturing a wholesome and kind approach to my mother's private practice. I designed, coded and published this website as my first solo project.

PAWS of Austin

The Paws of Austin website redesign focused on enhancing the online experience for a nonprofit dedicated to rescuing and rehoming large-breed dogs. The existing website struggled with outdated design, poor navigation, and a lack of clarity in key areas like donations and adoption.

QuestLink

QuestLink is a local social networking app designed for tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) and board game enthusiasts. It connects players, game masters, and event hosts in their communities, making it easy to organize games, find groups, and foster meaningful connections. With features tailored to diverse playstyles and experience levels, QuestLink simplifies game scheduling and encourages collaboration in the gaming community.

Money Moves

Money Moves is a financial literacy app designed to guide users through the process of building credit and effectively managing their money. The app offers a range of features, including educational modules, budgeting tools, and credit score tracking, all tailored to users at various stages of their financial journey.
