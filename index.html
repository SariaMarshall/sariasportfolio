Case Studies

Centered Mind Counseling

The Centered Mind Counseling website redesign focused on capturing a wholesome and kind approach to my mother's private practice. I designed, coded, and published this website as my first solo project.

PAWS of Austin

The PAWS of Austin redesign enhanced the online experience for a nonprofit rescuing and rehoming large-breed dogs, solving issues with outdated design, poor navigation, and unclear donation flows.

QuestLink

QuestLink is a local social networking app for TTRPG and board game fans. It connects players, game masters, and hosts to make organizing games easier and foster a sense of community.

Money Moves

Money Moves is a financial literacy app that teaches users how to build credit and manage money through interactive lessons, budgeting tools, and progress tracking.
