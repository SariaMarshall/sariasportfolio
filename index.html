Case Studies

Centered Mind Counseling

The Centered Mind Counseling website redesign captures a wholesome and kind approach to my mother’s private therapy practice. I designed, coded, and published this website as my first solo project.

PAWS of Austin

This redesign enhances the online experience for a nonprofit rescuing and rehoming large-breed dogs. The updated site improves navigation, structure, and donation flow.

QuestLink

QuestLink is a social networking app for tabletop and board game enthusiasts. It connects players, game masters, and event hosts to organize games and foster community connections.

Money Moves

Money Moves is a financial literacy app that helps users build credit, budget effectively, and develop long-term financial confidence through interactive education.
