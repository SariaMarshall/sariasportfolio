Money Moves

A financial literacy app designed to guide users through building credit and managing money.

Project Overview

Money Moves helps users learn about credit, budgeting, and financial independence. The goal was to create a friendly, approachable experience for people who may feel overwhelmed by finances.

Problem

  • Financial concepts can feel intimidating or unclear.
  • Most financial apps assume users already have knowledge.
  • People need simple, guided steps to build confidence.
