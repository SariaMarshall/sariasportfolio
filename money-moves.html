Money Moves – Financial Literacy App

UX/UI Case Study

Overview

Money Moves is a financial literacy app aimed at young adults who want to build credit, understand budgeting, and learn money basics through simple lessons.

My Role

  • UX/UI Designer
  • Concept Development
  • User Personas
  • Wireframes

Problem

Many young adults lack access to clear, judgment-free financial education. Apps that do exist can feel overwhelming or too advanced.

Solution

Money Moves guides users through core financial concepts using gamification, small lessons, and simple terminology that encourages learning without shame.

Wireframes

Outcome

Users reported feeling more confident about credit building and budgeting after completing the guided lessons.
