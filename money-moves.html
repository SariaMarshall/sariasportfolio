Money Moves

Overview Money Moves is a financial literacy app that helps users build credit, learn money basics, and track financial growth. It is aimed at users who feel overwhelmed by budgeting and credit management. Problem Many young adults struggle with: Understanding credit Creating healthy budgeting habits Knowing what impacts their score Knowing where to start Existing apps feel too complex or assume a high level of financial knowledge. My Role UX/UI Designer Research Wireframes User flows Process 1. Research I analyzed credit education platforms and budgeting apps. Most were overwhelming, number-heavy, or not beginner-friendly. 2. Persona Insights The target user needs: Simple explanations Bite-sized learning Clear progress tracking A judgment-free tone 3. Information Architecture I structured the app around three main pillars: Learn — Modules teaching financial concepts Build — Credit-building steps and reminders Track — Credit score and budgeting overview 4. Low-Fidelity Wireframes Features include: Learning paths Credit score simulation Budget builder Savings goals Rewards for completing modules Solution Money Moves creates an accessible, friendly introduction to financial literacy. Instead of overwhelming dashboards, it guides users one step at a time. Outcome Simplified financial education Clear pathways to better credit Encouraging, non-judgmental UX tone This project demonstrates my ability to handle educational UX and simplify complex topics
