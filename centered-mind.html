Centered Mind Counseling

Role: UX/UI Designer

Responsibilities: UX research, user flows, wireframing, prototyping, and UI design (end-to-end)

A warm, welcoming redesign for a trauma-focused private therapy practice.

Project Overview

Centered Mind Counseling is a private therapy practice specializing in trauma-informed care. The original website lacked warmth, clarity, and modern structure. My goal was to create a digital experience that reflected the therapist’s compassionate approach while making it easy for potential clients to find information and reach out.

Problem

Individuals seeking therapy often feel overwhelmed and vulnerable. The existing website was text-heavy, outdated, and difficult to navigate, failing to build emotional trust or clearly communicate services and values.

Solution

I designed a calming, accessible website using clear language, intentional white space, and a warm, nature-inspired color palette to reduce cognitive load and guide users toward confident next steps.

Goals

  • Create a modern, soft, and approachable visual identity
  • Improve clarity around services offered
  • Highlight trauma-informed care principles
  • Make the contact process simple and inviting

Visual Direction

The visual system uses calming greens and neutral tones paired with a mix of serif and sans-serif typography to balance professionalism with warmth and approachability.

Outcome

The final design presents a supportive, professional online presence that prioritizes emotional safety, clarity, and ease of navigation—helping users feel comfortable taking the first step toward care.
