Centered Mind Counseling

Website redesign focused on clarity, comfort, and trust.

Overview

Centered Mind Counseling is a private therapy practice specializing in trauma-informed care. The original website lacked structure, emotional warmth, and clear pathways for users to learn about services or contact the therapist.

My goal was to redesign the site to feel welcoming, grounded, and trustworthy — emphasizing emotional safety, accessibility, and ease of navigation.

My Role

  • UX/UI Designer
  • Researcher
  • Content Strategy
  • Front-End Developer (HTML/CSS)

Problem

Users struggled to understand what the practice offered, locate contact information, and feel emotionally connected to the brand. The site also lacked visual hierarchy and modern usability standards.

Solution

I designed a warm, modern layout using green and beige tones with clean serif/sans-serif typography. The redesign improves:

  • Clarity of services
  • Emotional comfort through visuals and tone
  • Direct pathways to contact the therapist
  • Navigation and readability
