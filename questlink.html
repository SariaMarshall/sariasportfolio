QuestLink

Role: UX/UI Designer

Responsibilities: UX research, user flows, wireframing, prototyping, and UI design end-to-end.

A local social networking app for TTRPG & board game enthusiasts.

Project Overview

QuestLink connects players, game masters, and event hosts in their local community. The app helps users find games, organize sessions, and connect through shared tabletop experiences.

Problem

Tabletop gamers lack a centralized, intuitive platform to find compatible players and events, often relying on scattered social channels that create coordination friction.

Solution

QuestLink was designed to match users based on play preferences, experience level, and location while streamlining event discovery and scheduling.

Outcome

The solution fosters stronger local gaming communities through thoughtful matching and simplified game organization.
