QuestLink – Local TTRPG Networking App

UX/UI Case Study

Overview

QuestLink is a local networking platform that connects tabletop RPG and board game players. It simplifies finding groups, hosting sessions, and joining events.

My Role

  • UX/UI Designer
  • Feature Mapping
  • User Flow Structure
  • Low-Fidelity Prototyping

Problem

Game lovers struggle to find local groups that match their playstyle, experience level, or schedule. Most solutions rely on scattered social media posts.

Solution

QuestLink organizes players, GMs, and events in one place with clear filters, messaging tools, and group-matching features.

Wireframes

Outcome

The prototype’s filtering and event-hosting features significantly improved group matching and reduced planning time.
