QuestLink

Overview QuestLink is a social networking app for tabletop RPG and board-game enthusiasts. It connects players, game masters, and event hosts in local communities. This project reached the low-fidelity prototype stage. Problem TTRPG players struggle with: Finding local groups Coordinating game sessions Matching playstyles Keeping track of campaigns Current platforms (Discord, Facebook groups, Meetup) are broad and not tailored to TTRPG needs. My Role UX Research User flows Low-fidelity wireframes Interaction structure Process 1. Research I conducted pattern analysis across: TTRPG forums Meetup Discord communities Player surveys Users consistently reported frustration around unreliable groups, scheduling, and mismatched playstyles. 2. User Goals Players want to: Find local groups quickly See who is a GM, player, or host Filter by playstyle, game type, and experience Avoid flaky or unsafe situations 3. Wireframing I mapped core flows: Onboarding with preferences Group discovery Game hosting Group chat & scheduling Player profiles 4. Low-Fidelity Prototype The prototype includes: A card-based group discovery screen A scheduling hub Event creation for hosts Clear tags for experience level and playstyle Solution QuestLink solves a real community problem by giving players one dedicated platform to connect, plan, and play. Even at the low-fidelity stage, the concept has strong potential for high usability and community impact.
